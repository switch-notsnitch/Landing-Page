Privacy Policy

At SWITCH, we care about your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it and how you control it.

1. What We Collect

Basic Info: Name, age, gender, department, year of study

Profile Details: Bio, interests, what you're looking for

Usage Data: Logins, app activity (e.g., matches, swipes)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Match you with relevant people
  • Show your profile to others (based on preferences)
  • Improve app functionality and fix bugs
  • Send relevant notifications

We never sell your personal information.

3. Who Can See Your Info?

  • Only users of the app can see your profile
  • You control what is visible (e.g., optional interests, intent)
  • We do not index your profile on search engines

4. Your Choices

  • You can edit your profile at any time
  • You can delete your account and all associated data
  • You can block or report users

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely. We follow industry standards to protect it.
